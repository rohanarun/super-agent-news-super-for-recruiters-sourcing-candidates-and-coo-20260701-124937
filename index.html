Why recruiting workflows break most AI assistants

Too many real tools

Recruiters live in LinkedIn, job boards, ATSs, calendars, email, and video interview tools. Chat‑only AI can suggest steps, but it can’t actually do the work.

Repetition is expensive

Sourcing, outreach follow‑ups, and interview coordination repeat every week. Without memory of past computer actions, agents cost the same every run.

Security risk is rising

Recent reporting shows how agentic tools that execute actions can be hijacked through poisoned inputs or workflows if not carefully designed.

Covered by securityweek.com and darkreading.com.
